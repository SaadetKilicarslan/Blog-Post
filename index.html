Handmade Soap Benefits

Handmade soap offers numerous benefits over commercial alternatives. Crafted with natural ingredients and essential oils, it is gentle on the skin and free from harsh chemicals, making it suitable for all skin types, including sensitive ones. Handmade soap retains glycerin, a natural moisturizer that commercial soaps often remove.
